About

I study emergent gauge phenomena in correlated magnets, with an emphasis on dipolar–octupolar quantum spin ice in Ce-based pyrochlores. Methodologically, I develop and deploy large-scale numerical tools—exact diagonalization (Lanczos/shift‑invert/TPQ), numerical linked‑cluster expansions, Monte Carlo and Landau–Lifshitz dynamics—optimized for modern CPU/GPU clusters.

Broadly, my goal is to connect microscopic models to measurable signatures (specific heat, static/dynamical spin structure factors, magnetization processes) and to map field–temperature phase diagrams with controlled approximations.
